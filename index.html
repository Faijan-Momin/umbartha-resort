About us



Umbartha resort located at Nane ghat is a holiday destination for travellers who enjoy silence, nature and food. From rammed earth walls to gardens full of local flora and lush green surroundings makes Umabartha an exotic location.

Nane Ghat is a mountain pass in the Western Ghats. During the reign of the Satavahana, Naneghat pass was one of the trade routes., connecting the Konkan coastal communities with Deccan high plateau through Junnar.
